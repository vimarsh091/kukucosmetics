Our Plant

KUKU Cosmetics’ manufacturing plant is a modern, state-of-the-art facility located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Equipped with advanced machinery, skilled professionals, and a strong R&D department, we ensure the production of high-quality cosmetics that meet international standards.

Our facility also features well-planned infrastructure and an eco-friendly work environment, enabling us to excel in the private labelling sector and deliver innovative, reliable products to our clients.

Highlights of our manufacturing plant

  • FDA approved R.C.C. production unit
  • Adherence to the GMP/ ISO/ USFDA standards of manufacturing
  • All manufacturing and filling areas maintained under class 1,00,000 (HEPA Filters) with vitrified tiles and all walls of production area are covered with glaze tiles, which ensures cleanliness and hygiene
  • Separate man and material entry air locks for each manufacturing area as well as filling rooms
  • All manufacturing and filling areas maintained under class 1, 00,000 (HEPA Filters) with vitrified tiles and all walls of production area covered with Glaze tiles, which ensures cleanliness and hygiene
  • FDA Approved Cosmetics Unit.
  • All Vessels, Machines and Contract Parts are Made with SS-316 To Prevent Material Reaction & To Maintain Batch Hygiene.
  • Segregation of every critical processing activity in each zone, to avoid cross-contamination
  • Each zone has separate dehumidification unit and dust extraction systems
  • Hi-tech laboratory setup for product innovation and testing
  • CCTV camera setup throughout the factory for complete safety and security
  • Respective zones, areas and even uniforms marked with specific colours to ensure total segregation
  • Duo Pass Reverse Osmosis (RO) water system/ de-mineralization plant
  • Zero-discharge Effluent Treatment Plant
  • Fully computerized individual/ separate office administrative building with latest communication facilities along with server room

Accreditation

KUKU Cosmetics’ manufacturing facilities operate in strict accordance with government norms and regulations. Our plant and laboratories comply with GMP, WHO-GMP, ISO 9001:2015, and are USFDA-registered.

All products manufactured at KUKU Cosmetics are approved by the FDA, Gujarat, ensuring safety, quality, and consistency in every batch we produce.

Research & Innovation

KUKU Cosmetics houses a modern, in-house Research & Innovation Laboratory equipped for testing raw materials, packaging materials, in-process materials, and finished products.

Our dedicated R&D team brings together some of the brightest minds in the industry, working to create customized formulations and samples tailored to meet the unique requirements of each client.
